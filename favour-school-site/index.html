Pursuit of Academic Excellence

About Us

At Favour Complex School, we are dedicated to providing a well-rounded education that prepares students for success in all aspects of life. Our mission is to provide a strong foundation of moral and academic excellence, delivering a relevant curriculum that adapts to the changing times.

Our Mission Statement

To provide a strong foundation of moral and academic excellence; relevant curriculum and responsive to the changing of time within a safe attractive and caring environment that challenges each pupil to fulfill his/her potentials.

Our Vision Statement

To be one great institution that prepares, motivates and inspires every child to learn by instilling in them critical thinking skills, quality education and respect for core values of honesty, loyalty, perseverance and compassion. Pupils will have success for today and be prepared for tomorrow.

Our Core Values

  • Follows Excellence: We strive for excellence in all aspects of school life, encouraging students to reach their full potential.
  • Applies Effective Learning Strategies: We promote effective learning strategies that help students achieve academic success.
  • Values the Concerns of Parents: We believe in working closely with parents to support student growth and development.
  • Occupies Learners in Quality Work Activities: We engage learners in quality work activities that promote academic excellence and prepare them for future challenges.
  • Upholds Christian Values and Hard Work: We promote Christian values and a strong work ethic, encouraging students to develop a sense of responsibility and integrity.
  • Recuperates Academic Performance in the Society: We aim to improve academic performance and make a positive impact in our society.

Our Educational Philosophy

At Favour Complex School, we believe that every child is unique and deserving of a quality education that prepares them for success in all aspects of life. Our educational philosophy is centered around the following principles:

  • Student-Centered Learning: We believe in putting students at the center of the learning process, encouraging them to take an active role in their education.
  • Academic Excellence: We strive for academic excellence in all subjects, promoting critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity.
  • Character Development: We believe in developing the whole child, promoting values such as honesty, loyalty, and compassion.

Our Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to provide students with a well-rounded education that prepares them for success in all aspects of life. We offer a range of subjects, including:

Montessori Program

Our preschool program uses the Montessori method to foster early childhood development, encouraging independence, creativity, and self-directed learning.

GES Curriculum

Our curriculum aligns with the Ghana Education Service standards, ensuring students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for future success.

Our Facilities

Our school facilities are designed to provide a safe, attractive, and caring environment that promotes learning and development. Our facilities include:

Classrooms

Our classrooms are equipped with modern teaching aids and technology, providing students with a conducive learning environment.

Our library is stocked with a range of books and resources, promoting reading and research skills.

Playground

Our playground is designed to promote physical activity and outdoor play, essential for healthy development.

Our Faculty

Our faculty is comprised of experienced and qualified teachers who are dedicated to providing students with a quality education. Our teachers are passionate about teaching and learning, and are committed to helping students achieve their full potential.

Admissions

We welcome applications from students who share our values and are committed to academic excellence. Our admissions process is designed to ensure that students are placed in the right program and receive the support they need to succeed.
